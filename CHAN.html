Clone Hero Anime

The biggest pack of anime music since the release of Clone Hero. Featuring more than 60 anime songs spanning all across openings, endings and even OST tracks! The setlist is built with thematical and difficulty parameters in mind.

Featuring anime like Naruto, Assassination Classroom, To-Love RU, K-ON, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure, Love Live and many more!
